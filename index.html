SpinCheck Wheel
About the tool

Hey there,

Use checkins and checkout prompts to just lighten the mood, or even help to build rapport within your team.

The SpinCheck Wheel was built to help you on this task offering a variety of curated practical, reflexive and creative prompts.

How to use it

Click in the wheel to spin it and get a diferent prompt. You can either get a different prompt for each person, or you can just spin for a single prompt and all people could share about that same prompt.

You can also see the full list of questions, so you can pick up at your convenience.

How to reuse it

You can find the source code of the tool available in github. Feel free to share, copy, remix, transform, and build upon the material.
