About the tool

Hey there,

The SpinCheck Wheel was built to help you on the task of having a variety of curated focused, reflexive and creative prompts for meetings.

Use checkins and checkout prompts to just lighten the mood, or even help to build rapport within your team.

How to use it

Click in the wheel to spin it and get a prompt. You can either get a new prompt for each person, or you can use a single prompt for everyone.

You can also navigate trhough the full list of prompts, so you can also pick up prompt at your convenience.
